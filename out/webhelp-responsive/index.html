Main Introduction
How to Talk to Someone
In order to create a true connection with someone, you want to be able to talk to them and learn about that person. These instrutctions will teach to how to talk to a person respectfully and try to become their friend.
How to Dress Friendly
In order to be an approachable person, you must be dressed to impress. While clothes should not be the defining factor of who you are, it can definately show off your style and personality. This guide will help you to dress well and find new connections and friends and focuses on looking friendly to most people.
How to Make a College Breakfast
Most college students will not have any time to have a complex breakfast. These instructions are to help with creating the most simple breakfast that a person could make, so they can still get the energy they need to work through the day!
